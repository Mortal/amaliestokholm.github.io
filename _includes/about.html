About

Hi! I'm Amalie and I am an astronomy graduate student studying at the Stellar Astrophysics Centre at Aarhus University, Denmark. My primary research interest is the field of Galactic archaeology where by studying the demographics of stars we can learn about the history and evolution of our Milky Way.
Outside of work, I enjoy running, messing around with small coding projects, and drinking a beer or two with friends. If you would like to know more about my work, including my published papers, please keep scrolling!
