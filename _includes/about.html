About

Hi! I'm Amalie and I am a postdoctural researcher in astrophysics studying the stars in our own galaxy, the Milky Way. I am current part of the Asterochronometry group at Università di Bologna in Bologna, Italy. My primary research interest is the field of Galactic archaeology where we learn about the history and evolution of the Milky Way by studying the demographics of its stars. I did my PhD at the iSIMBA group at the Stellar Astrophysics Centre at Aarhus University, Denmark, where I also was heavily involved with the BAyesian STellar Algorithm (BASTA).
